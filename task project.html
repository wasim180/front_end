My Favorite Book: "The Great Gatsby"

"The Great Gatsby" is a novel written byF.scott Fitzgerald and was first published in 1925. It is widely considered one of the greatest American novels.The story is set during theRoaring Twenties and explores themesof love,wealth,and the American Dream.

The novel's main character,Jay Gatsby,is famous for his extravagant parties and unrequited love for Daisy Buchanan.His story ultimately leads to tragic consequences. The book is known for its symbolismand its crictical veiw of the American dream.
